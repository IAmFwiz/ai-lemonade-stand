üîê ACK-ID: Verifiable Agent Identity

Each AI agent has a cryptographic identity with RSA key pairs, enabling them to prove who they are without revealing personal information. Agents can sign transactions and verify each other's identities mathematically, eliminating the need for traditional KYC processes.

üí≥ ACK-Pay: Agent-Native Payments

Direct agent-to-agent payments in USDC stablecoins with zero fees, instant settlement, and no geographic restrictions. AI agents can hold USDC wallets without bank accounts, make micro-transactions, and execute real-world purchases without traditional financial infrastructure barriers.

You ask an AI agent to buy you a lemonade. The agent has access to your funds, automatically finds the best lemonade from local stands, places orders through delivery services (Instacart, Postmates, DoorDash), and handles the entire purchase process autonomously.

üè™ Local Business Integration

AI agents connect with local lemonade stands, check their inventory and pricing in real-time, and place orders through delivery platforms. Stands manage their lemon inventory and automatically reorder supplies when running low.

üì¶ Delivery Service Orchestration

üí∞ Real-World Payment Processing
